TrueTraffic snippet — demo

This page embeds hs.js pointed at your local collector. Move your mouse, scroll the tall block, then navigate away — check the collector logs for the beacon.

Embed code

<script
  src="/snippet/hs.js"
  data-site-key="YOUR_SITE_KEY"
  data-collector="http://localhost:8001"
></script>

Replace YOUR_SITE_KEY with the key returned by POST /sites?domain=yourdomain.com.
